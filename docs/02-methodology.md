## Methodology

This project follows the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) across all five functions:
- Identify
- Protect
- Detect
- Respond
- Recover

Risk assessment was conducted by:
- Identifying key business assets
- Identifying threats and vulnerabilities
- Evaluating impact and likelihood using qualitative risk scoring
- Mapping risks to controls aligned with NIST CSF, ISO 27001, and CIS controls

All assessmentsare are based on publicly available information and industry-standard SaaS assumptions.
